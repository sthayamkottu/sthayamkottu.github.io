My research combines process-based modeling, data analysis, and field observations to understand ecosystem responses to environmental change.

## Research themes

- **Carbon and water cycles**: improving process representation and uncertainty quantification.
- **Climate-change impacts**: evaluating ecosystem vulnerability under future climate scenarios.
- **Model-data integration**: combining observations and models to improve predictive performance.

## Current directions

- Developing robust workflows for reproducible earth system science.
- Building collaborative projects that connect modeling, remote sensing, and experiments.
- Translating research outputs into open, reusable tools and datasets.
